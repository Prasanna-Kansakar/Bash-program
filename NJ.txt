
Neymar Jr.:
Neymar da Silva Santos Junior known as Neymar, is a Brazilian professional footballer 
who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the Brazil national team. 
Neymar came into prominence at Santos, where he made his professional debut aged 17. 
With 64 goals in 103 matches for Brazil since debuting at age 18, 
Neymar is the second highest goalscorer for his national team, trailing only Pele.
Neymar was included by Time in its list of the 100 most influential people in the world.
