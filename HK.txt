
Harry Kane:
Harry Edward Kane MBE is an English professional footballer who plays as a striker 
for Premier League club Tottenham Hotspur and captains the England national team. 
Regarded as one of the best strikers in the world, Kane is known for his 
prolific goalscoring record and ability to link play.
In February 2020, he became Tottenham's second-highest all-time goalscorer in official competitions.
Kane has scored 33 goals in 52 games for England.
